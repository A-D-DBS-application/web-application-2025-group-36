Bridging the Gap Between Science & Industry

At REVIEWR, we help researchers share their insights and help companies find the scientific knowledge they need — faster and more efficiently.

Get Started Learn More

Our Vision

We believe that knowledge becomes truly powerful only when it is shared. Thousands of valuable papers exist, yet companies often fail to find them — or find them too late. Researchers struggle to make an impact outside the academic world. We want to bridge this gap.

Our Mission

To build a platform that connects researchers and companies so that:

  • companies can find relevant scientific insights more quickly,
  • researchers can increase their real-world impact,
  • collaborations form and grow more easily,
  • innovation accelerates at scale.

The Future

We are building an ecosystem where science and industry naturally find each other, powered by intelligent matching, transparent reviews, and a strong community.
