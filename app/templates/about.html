About Us

REVIEWR was founded with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between academic research and companies. Every year, thousands of brilliant papers are published, yet only a fraction reaches the industry.

  • Researchers gain more visibility.
  • Companies find relevant knowledge faster.
  • Society benefits from better innovation.
